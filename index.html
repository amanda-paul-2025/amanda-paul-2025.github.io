Things to do nearby

The South Bay has a lot to offer, and here is just a small sampling of things to check out while you are in town.

Hiking/Outdoor activities

Santa Teresa County Park

Amanda's backyard park features lots of great biking and hiking trails.

Almaden Quicksilver County Park

A large park with lots of hiking and biking trails, along with a long and interesting history of mercury mining. At their peak, the mines in this region were the second most productive in the world.

Henry Coe State Park

The largest state park in Northern California, Henry Coe features miles of rugged trails in the Diablo Mountain Range. Summer temperatures here can be hot, so be prepared if visiting during the day.

Local attractions

Gilroy Gardens

A family-friendly amusement park featuring fun rides and extensive botanical gardens.

Andy's Orchard

The best dried fruit around, period. Paul and Kathy Hansell (Amanda's maternal grandmother) are both big fans of this place.

Places to eat

Super Taqueria

Low-key, but very solid Mexican food.

Aqui

Very tasty and creative Cal-Mex dishes with lots of good veggie options.

Maurizio's Restaurant

A Hansell family favorite for Italian food.

In-N-Out

Paul thinks their fries are great; Amanda, not so much. Decide for yourself!
